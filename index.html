The WHO recognizes the next values like a reference standard and help people to take action by their own health.

Clasification BMI kg/m^2 Risk
Normal 18.5 - 24.9 Average
Overweight 25.0 - 29.9 Increased
Obesity lv I 30.0 - 34.9 Moderate
Obesity lv II 35.0 - 39.9 Severe
Obesity lv III >40.0 Very severe
Taken from WHO
